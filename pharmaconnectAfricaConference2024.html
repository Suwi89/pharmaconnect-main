PharmaConnect is an annually convened conference that brings together major players and stakeholders in pharmaceutical manufacturing, access to medicines and private sector engagement, regulatory sciences and quality assurance, pharmacy governance and policy, pharmacovigilance and medicine safety, supply chain and last mile and geographical access, and the adherence and the medicine use process. The conference prioritizes attendance by government representatives, local players in the industry, academia, and Implementing partners with regional presence.

The conference will be held from 20th to 22nd August, 2024, at the Radisson Blu Hotel, in Lusaka, Zambia
