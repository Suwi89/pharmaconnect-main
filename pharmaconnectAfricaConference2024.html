PharmaConnect Africa Conference 2024

Market access in Africa – what are the catalysts for change?

Join us at PharmaConnect Africa Conference 2024! This conference is a gathering point for influential figures in the pharmaceutical industry, convening key stakeholders across various sectors. Participants will include leaders in pharmaceutical manufacturing, medicine access, private sector engagement, regulatory sciences, quality assurance, pharmacy governance, policy, pharmacovigilance, medicine safety, and supply chain management. We also ensure strong representation from government officials, industry leaders, academia, and implementing partners with a regional presence.

Last year, we welcomed over 150 delegates from 17 countries, featuring notable organizations such as WHO and SAHPRA. This year, our goals are set even higher!

Call for abstracts !

Abstract submissions for both oral presentations and poster displays are now open.

Abstract submission guidelines

We invite you to contribute to the PharmaConnect Africa Conference 2024 by submitting an abstract that aligns with our theme: "Market Access in Africa: What are the Catalysts for Change?" and pertinent to one of our subthemes:

  • Regulatory harmonization
  • Manufacturing in Africa
  • Medicine use & service delivery
  • Supply chain innovations, including the impact of AI on market access and distribution.

Submission Details:

  • Word limit: Abstracts must not exceed 300 words.
  • Submission deadline: 31 May 2024
  • Notification of acceptance: If your abstract is selected, you will be notified by 14 June 2024.

We encourage originality and relevance to the conference themes. Please ensure your abstracts are concise and reflect critical analysis of the topics. Accepted abstracts will contribute significantly to our discussions, helping shape the future of pharmaceutical access in Africa.
